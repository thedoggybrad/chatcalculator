ChatCalculator


A calculator in a Chat

Greetings! This is a basic 4 mathematical operations calculator on a chat. The format for sending your equation, is like "9 + 11", and to ensure that your equations are well understand, Only use the symbols "+, -, * or /".
Avoid adding unnecessary characters to your equation like the equals sign or the software may refuse to respond.
