Calculator in a chat

Hello there! This is Chat Calculator, I can solve any basic mathematical equations for you, just like a traditional basic calculator that you can now experience in a chat format. I can only process the 4 basic operations, decimal and integer values. Only by using the operators (+, -, * and /), with the use of words/letters, quotes and other unnecessary characters are prohibited. Kindly observe proper spacing on the numbers, noting that spacing between numbers and operations are not spacing sensitive.

Example of allowed entries: 9+9, 9 - 9, 9* 9 or 9 /9

ChatCalculator does not restrict the number of equational requests that are processed.
