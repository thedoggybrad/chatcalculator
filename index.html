This is a basic calculator that performs the four mathematical operations.

To use the calculator, enter your equation in the format "9 + 11".

Only use the symbols "+", "-", "*", or "/" for operations.

Avoid adding unnecessary characters like the equals sign (=).

Integers and decimals are supported.
